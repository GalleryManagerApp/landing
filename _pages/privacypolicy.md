# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how **Gallery Manager** ("the app", "we", "us", or "our") handles your information. We built Gallery Manager with a privacy-first approach: the app is designed to work entirely on your device, and we do not collect, sell, or share your personal content.

By using the app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

<br>

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Content processed on your device
To find duplicate or similar photos, videos, contacts, and calendar events, Gallery Manager needs access to the relevant data on your device. This access is requested through the standard iOS permission prompts.

**All of this processing happens locally, on your device.** Your photos, videos, contacts, and calendar data are **never uploaded, transmitted, or stored on our servers**, and we never see them.

### 1.2 Information you provide to us
If you contact us by email for support, we receive the information you choose to share (such as your email address and the contents of your message). We use it only to respond to your request.

### 1.3 Information we do not collect
We do not collect your name, location, photos, videos, contacts, or calendar entries. We do not require you to create an account.

<br>

## 2. Permissions We Request

| Permission | Why it is used |
| :--- | :--- |
| Photos | To scan for duplicate, similar, or oversized photos and videos so you can review and remove them. |
| Contacts | To detect and help merge duplicate contacts. |
| Calendar | To find duplicate or spam calendar events. |
| Network | To run the optional built-in internet speed test. |

You can grant or revoke these permissions at any time in **iOS Settings → Gallery Manager**. The app does not function in areas where you have declined the corresponding permission, but you remain free to use the rest.

<br>

## 3. Third-Party Services

Gallery Manager does not include third-party advertising or analytics SDKs that profile you. Purchases and subscriptions are processed by **Apple** through the App Store; we never receive or store your payment details. Apple's handling of that data is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

<br>

## 4. Data Security

Because your content stays on your device, it is protected by your device's own security (passcode, Face ID / Touch ID, and iOS encryption). We do not maintain servers that store your personal content, which means there is no remote database of your data to be breached.

<br>

## 5. Children's Privacy

Gallery Manager is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

<br>

## 6. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store your personal content, there is no personal data held by us to access, correct, or delete. You remain in full control of the data on your device and can revoke the app's permissions or delete the app at any time. For any privacy-related questions, you may contact us using the details below.

<br>

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
