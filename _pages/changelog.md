# Changelog

A history of updates to Gallery Manager.

<br>

### `Initial Release`
# **Version 1.0**
The first release of Gallery Manager.

#### Features
- Smart cleanup of duplicate and similar photos.
- Detection of identical and oversized videos.
- Merging of duplicate contacts.
- Cleanup of duplicate and spam calendar events.
- Built-in internet speed test.
- Storage insights and on-device AI suggestions.
- Fully on-device processing — your data never leaves your phone.
