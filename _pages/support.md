## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is my data safe?
Yes. Gallery Manager processes everything **on-device** — your photos, videos, contacts and calendar data never leave your phone.

### Does deleting duplicates remove my originals?
No. The app helps you identify duplicate and similar items, but you always stay in control — nothing is deleted without your confirmation.

### How do I manage or cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are handled through your Apple ID. Open **Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions** on your device to view, change or cancel your plan.

### I found a bug or have a feature request
Great — we'd love to hear it! Email us at **[gallerymanagerapp@gmail.com](mailto:gallerymanagerapp@gmail.com)** and we'll take a look.
